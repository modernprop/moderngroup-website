Our Story

From Jersey's
hospitality to its
skyline.

The Modern Group has been a trusted name in Jersey since 1967. We began as the island's foremost hospitality operator — running hotels, restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues across Jersey and the United Kingdom for over five decades.

In 2021, we undertook a deliberate strategic transformation, deploying the capital and expertise built through decades of operations into real estate and investments — sectors we believe offer long-term, resilient returns.

Today we own, operate, and manage a growing portfolio of residential, commercial, and retail assets, alongside a select investment programme in innovative private companies.
